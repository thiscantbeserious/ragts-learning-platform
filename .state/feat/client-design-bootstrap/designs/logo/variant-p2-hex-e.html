Variant P2: Hex-E

The hexagonal gate shape adjusted to read as the letter "E". The left side forms a vertical spine. Three horizontal strokes extend right with angled hex-style tips, creating openings that read as an E while maintaining the geometric, hexagonal character. Lives inside the same 28px branded box as BrandMark and HexGateIcon.
