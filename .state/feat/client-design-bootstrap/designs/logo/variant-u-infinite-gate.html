Variant U: Infinite Gate

The hexagonal gate meets the infinity symbol — the never-ending learning loop. The right side of the hex dissolves into a lemniscate (figure-8) curve that flows out through the portal gap, loops, and returns. It embodies Erika's core concept: "Loop and Loop — round and round until you understand." The gate is where human insight enters; the infinity loop is the continuous refinement cycle.
