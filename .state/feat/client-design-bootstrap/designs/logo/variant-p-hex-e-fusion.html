Variant P: Hex-E Fusion

The hexagonal gate shape morphed so it reads as both a hexagon AND the letter "E". The left side forms the vertical spine, while the top, middle, and bottom segments become the three horizontal strokes of an E. The right side remains open -- preserving the portal/gate gap and creating the distinctive openings of the letter.
