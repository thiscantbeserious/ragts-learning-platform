Design Rationale

The Reflection Gate evolves the hex gate nav icon into a brand mark by mirroring it across a central axis. The left gate is the primary — full opacity, representing the human's direct experience reviewing an agent session. The right gate is the reflection — reduced to 45% opacity, representing what the human sees when they look through the portal: the agent's perspective, echoed back.

The gap between the two gates is the central concept. It is not empty space — it is the threshold where reflection happens. The subtle center line at large sizes hints at this boundary. At small sizes (28px, 16px), the gap segments and center line are dropped for clarity, leaving just the two mirrored open-hex silhouettes.

The logo directly maps to the platform's vision: "Loop and Loop — round and round until you understand." The mirrored forms suggest a cycle. The gap suggests the moment of pause — the reflection step that makes the difference between repetition and learning.
