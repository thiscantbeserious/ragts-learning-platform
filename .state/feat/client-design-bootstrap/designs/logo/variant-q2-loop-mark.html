Variant Q2: Loop Mark

The hex gate shape with one segment that curves into a loop arc, representing the asynchronous refinement loop at the heart of Erika's vision. The base is still the partial hexagon — five straight segments — but the top-right area where the "gap" lives becomes a flowing curve that loops back on itself. Angular meets curved. Structured meets flowing.
