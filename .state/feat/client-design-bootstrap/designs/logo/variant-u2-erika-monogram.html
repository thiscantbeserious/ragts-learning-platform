Variant U2: Erika Monogram

E and K fused into a single geometric monogram, built from hex gate DNA. The E's spine and arms use the hex gate's straight segments. The K's diagonals shoot out at angles that echo 60-degree hex geometry. A dim gap-bridge connects them — the same visual language as the hex gate's missing segment. Abstract enough to be a mark, legible enough to read as "EK" once you see it.
