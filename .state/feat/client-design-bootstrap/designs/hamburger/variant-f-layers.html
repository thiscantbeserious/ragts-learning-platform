Variant F: Stacked Layers

F1 uses three overlapping rectangles in a diagonal cascade — back to front, each progressively more opaque. This "deck of cards" metaphor signals "hidden content behind this trigger." On hover, the layers fan apart to preview the reveal motion. F2 simplifies to two cards with a small chevron ">" on the front card, combining the stack metaphor with a directional navigation hint. Both are purely geometric and work with any accent color.
