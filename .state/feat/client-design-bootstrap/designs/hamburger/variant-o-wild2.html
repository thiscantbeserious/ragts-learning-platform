Design Rationale

Three nodes in a triangular formation, connected by synaptic lines — a neural network in miniature. The idle state has staggered node pulses suggesting active computation. On toggle, the nodes scatter outward (the network "fires") while the connection lines reform into an X.

Why it works for Erika: AI agents ARE neural networks. The synapse metaphor is literal — you're opening a view into AI agent sessions. The triangle formation is stable and recognizable at 28px. The scatter-to-X transition feels organic yet precise. Fully geometric, no text, whitelabel-safe.
