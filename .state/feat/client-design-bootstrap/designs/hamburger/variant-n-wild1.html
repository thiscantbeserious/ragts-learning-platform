Design Rationale

The dimensional portal metaphor maps to the act of "opening a view into another space" — the session sidebar. Two concentric ellipses read as a wormhole or portal viewed head-on. The idle breathing animation gives it life. On activation, the rings flatten and cross, suggesting the portal has been breached. The core dot dissolves as the passage opens.

Why it works for Erika: The platform is about observing AI agents working in terminal sessions — like peering through a portal into another dimension of computation. The geometric precision (ellipses, clean rotations) fits the TRON aesthetic. No text, no brand letter — purely geometric and fully whitelabel-safe.
