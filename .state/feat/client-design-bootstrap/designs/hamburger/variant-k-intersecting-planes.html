Variant K: Intersecting Planes

Three thin geometric shapes (parallelograms) overlapping at different angles -- stacked data planes, like architectural cross-sections or layered terminal buffers. On toggle, the skewed planes un-skew and converge into a clean X with staggered M3 glow pulse morph. The middle plane fades out while outer planes rotate into position.
