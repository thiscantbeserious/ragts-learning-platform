Variant C: Stylized E

C1 uses the identical "E" letter from the desktop BrandMark. On mobile, it acts as both brand identifier and menu toggle — users learn "tap E to navigate." C2 deconstructs the E into geometric bars with a vertical spine, making the hamburger metaphor explicit while retaining the E shape. The middle bar is shorter, breaking the uniform hamburger pattern to evoke the letter E.
