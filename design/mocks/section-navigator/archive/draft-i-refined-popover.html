Draft I: Refined Circular Popovers

Floating circuit nodes with hover popover cards -- no container box

I1: Floating Circuit Nodes

Round numbered pills floating on the right edge with no visible container. A dashed PCB trace connects them. Each pill is a circuit node soldered onto the screen edge. Popover card appears to the left on hover -- clean elevated surface style.
