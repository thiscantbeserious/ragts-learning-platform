Draft H: Three Radical Designs

Scrollbar Track / Radial Dial / Typographic Waterfall

H1: Scrollbar-Integrated Track

The navigator IS the scrollbar. A narrow segmented track where each section occupies vertical space proportional to its line count. The active segment glows. Gaps between segments are section boundaries. Hover floats a label to the left. No separate aside needed -- the scrollbar is the map. Width: 20px (track + chrome). Proportional segments give an instant sense of where the "heavy" sections live.

H2: Radial Dial

A compact circular dial where sections are distributed as arc segments around the ring perimeter. The active section's arc glows. The center displays the current section number and a truncated label. Marker sections use the secondary accent. Feels like a HUD targeting reticle. Fixed size: 120x120px regardless of section count -- density increases as sections increase.

H3: Typographic Waterfall

No shapes, no dots, no icons. Just section names stacked vertically in a narrow column. The active section name glows at full brightness with an accent left border. Nearby sections are slightly visible. Distant sections fade to near-invisible -- a ghostly waterfall of text that reveals the session shape through typography alone. Hover shows the full label. Fixed width: 72px.
