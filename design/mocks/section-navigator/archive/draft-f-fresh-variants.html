Draft F: Fresh Variants

3 new concepts + 3 refinements of existing work

F1 -- Horizontal Minimap Bar

A horizontal strip above the content. Each segment is proportional to section line count. Hovering shows a tooltip below. Active segment highlighted. Works like a genome browser or audio waveform overview — you see the shape of the whole session at once.

F2 -- Collapsible Cluster Groups

Optimized for 50+ sections. Marker sections act as group headers (wider, pink). Detected sections nest under them as smaller pills. Groups collapse to hide children, showing only the marker pill + a count badge. At 50 sections, only the active group is expanded. Fixed 48px width. Tooltip on hover.

F3 -- Minimal Tick Marks

No separate aside at all. Tiny tick marks sit flush on the right edge of the content area. Each tick represents a section. Marker ticks are pink and slightly wider. Active tick glows. Hover to see tooltip. Absolute minimum chrome — the content area loses zero horizontal space. Count badge at top-right corner.

F6 -- Circuit Trace Dots

Refinement of Draft C's circuit board aesthetic at fixed 36px width. No numbers visible — just dots on a glowing neon trace line. Dot color encodes type (cyan = detected, pink = marker). Dot brightness encodes density (dim = small section, bright = large). Active dot is larger and pulses. Hovering shows a tooltip with number, title, and metadata. The trace line itself glows near the active position. Pure TRON/cyberpunk feel.
